My Origins

I'm proudly Mexican/American I was raised in Mexico, in beautiful village named Cuquio in the state of Jalisco. Living there provided me a lot of importante live expiriences and skills. I loved living in Cuquio for it natural wonders, kind people and unique traditions. Although I was raised in Mexico, I was born in Texas, where I also shared the love for the American culture.


Family

My familY is originally from Cuquio, one of the most importante things in my life is my family and loved ones. When ever I had time to visit them, we all hang out in the plaza to eat ice-cream, enjoy a soccer game, eat some fresh fruit with chily or just enjoy the fountain view.
